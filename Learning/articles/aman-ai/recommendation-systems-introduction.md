unique role in refining and tailoring recommendations to meet both user and business requirements retrieval the retrieval stage serves to generate a pool of candidates using methods such as matrix the system arranges items into an ordered list for the user although scoring assigns relevance scores to each item the ordering stage allows further adjustments to ensure a diverse mix of
