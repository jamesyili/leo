**Key Steps Now to Ensure Promotion in 2026: The Year of AI**

Watch on [YouTube.](https://youtu.be/piGJzmIw7_I?si=rXFRXXDoPpXRvgTE)

In this talk, you’ll learn the principles that will not change in the Age of AI, how to re-equip your career with AI (the systematic how to pivot), and what your plan B looks like. 

**4 career actions to take now**

**1. Key requirements for leadership promotions**

***Need*** — is there an opening?

Comes from growth, reorgs, and departures (other people leave).

***Qualifications*** — do you have the skills?

Find out if you meet the next level requirements by reading written company documentation and getting feedback from your manager and skip.

***Selection*** — do “they” choose you?

**2. Write down an *****actual***** plan by month**

Organize your plan by What, When, Who:

***What*** do you need to do?

By ***when*** do you need to do it?

***Who*** do you need to convince?

***Who*** signs off?

Do this in partnership with your manager. Ensure they are aware and aligned —reinforce progress and next steps in your 1:1’s. 

**3. AI’s impact on promotions**

**Two consequences:**

Flatter orgs (you must be “hands-on” with AI). 

Rise of the “Super ICs”.

**Three levels of AI usage and whether they set you apart:**

Basic: Use AI to do more, faster (e.g. feed data files, write memos, summarize meetings). 

Advanced: Use AI to do things you could not before (e.g. visual design, identify new product features, work across languages). 

More Advanced: Use AI for “everything” (e.g. start everything with AI and if you do not know how to do it…have AI teach you). A subset is building your own agents to do work while you sleep. 

**4. What is your Plan B?**

**The future is more about working for yourself vs working for companies who think they can get you back in-office then use AI to automate your job.**

Companies already announced 2026 layoffs and we expect it to continue all year ([free resources](https://levelupwithethanevans.substack.com/p/layoffs-will-continue-all-year) to help you prepare and for those impacted). 

Ensure your resume and LinkedIn are up to date.

Add value and stay on top of your network. 

Start (or grow) a side hustle that generates income and/or upskills you.

Build your financial savings safety net now. 

**Expand your 2026 “career” plan to be your “life” plan:**

Career:

Projects that prove your skill level and establish your performance.

Key relationships to build and maintain. 

Goals for the year, when you need to hit them (be date specific) and who needs to be involved. 

Finances.

Health.

Family.

Purpose (e.g. what fulfills you, what path are you on).
