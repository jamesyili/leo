### 1) Own a flagship business outcome: Retentive Recommendations / retention lift
- **[Rationale]** Highest-leverage path to Director-caliber impact: measurable outcomes + cross-team alignment + durable mechanism.
- **[What "done" looks like]**
  - Clear strategy + roadmap that multiple orgs buy into (HF CG + Blending + UU / Growth partners).
  - One or more launches with measurable retention/engagement wins, and a durable mechanism (not a one-off).
- **[Constraints]**
  - Cross-org dependency complexity; shifting leadership landscape post-reorg.

- **[Leading signals]**
  - Exec/Dir asks move from "what are you doing?" to "how can I help unblock this?"
  - Pre-alignment becomes easier; fewer surprises in reviews.
  - Other teams proactively offer resources / adoption (pull vs push).
- **[Lagging metrics]**
  - Retention metric(s) relevant to the initiative (WAU/MAU, exact KPI per internal definitions).
  - Downstream engagement health (repins, saves, sessions) without quality regressions.
- **[Risks]**
  - Execution risk: strategy exists but doesn't convert to launches with measurable impact.
  - Resource risk: becoming the glue (over-involved), reducing scalability.
- **[Mitigations]**
  - Tight "exec story" (problem -> bet -> mechanism -> expected lift -> proof plan).
  - Named owners per workstream; act as integrator, not executor.
  - Define falsifiable milestones (what must be true by Date X).
- **[Confidence]** Medium-High.
